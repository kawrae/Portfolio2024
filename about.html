About Me

Corey Black

I am a passionate full stack developer with a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end technologies. My experience spans a wide range of programming languages and tools, making me a versatile and adaptive problem solver.

On the front-end, I am proficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, building responsive and intuitive user interfaces. I have worked extensively with Java, leveraging its capabilities to create dynamic web applications. Additionally, my background in game development with C# allows me to approach coding challenges creatively and efficiently.

On the Android development side, I am well-versed in Android Studio and Kotlin, building robust mobile applications tailored to the platform's unique needs.

In terms of back-end development, I have extensive experience in creating and managing databases, with strong proficiency in SQL and working knowledge of PHP. I understand the importance of building reliable, secure, and scalable systems that support smooth user experiences.

Whether working on front-end or back-end projects, I strive to bridge the gap between technology and user experience, delivering high-quality results that meet both functional and aesthetic goals.
